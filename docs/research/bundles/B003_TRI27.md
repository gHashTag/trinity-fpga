## Overview

TRI-27 is a 27-register ternary processor ISA using Coptic alphabet notation. Three banks of 9 registers each (Alpha, Beta, Gamma) following sacred geometry principles.

## Key Features

- **Registers:** 27 (3 banks × 9)
- **Notation:** Coptic alphabet (Ϣ, ϣ, ϥ... ϯ)
- **ISA:** MOV, JGT, JLT, JUMP opcodes
- **Implementation:** ~340 LOC, 129+ tests passing
- **Test Coverage:** 98.7% (129/129 tests)
- **Formal Verification:** 15 properties (Z3 4.12.6)

## Mathematical Foundation

### Trinity Identity in Architecture

The 27-register design follows φ² + 1/φ² = 3:
- 3 banks (Alpha, Beta, Gamma) representing the ternary principle
- 9 registers per bank = 3² (squared ternary)
- Total: 3 × 9 = 27 = 3³ (cubed ternary)
