## Production Notes

### Camera Settings
- Resolution: 1080p (1920×1080)
- Frame rate: 30fps or 60fps
- Focus: Manual, locked on board
- Lighting: Soft white LED from side-left

### Terminal Settings
- Font: JetBrains Mono 14pt
- Colors: Black bg, green text (retro style)
- Window size: 80×25 min

### Editing Notes
- No transitions between shots (hard cuts only)
- Text overlays should use box-drawing characters (╔═╗║╚╝)
- All overlays should fade in (0.2s) and fade out (0.2s)
- Keep diagrams simple — less is more

### Export Settings
- Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Bitrate: 8-10 Mbps
- Audio: AAC 192kbps
- Duration target: 45-60 seconds max
